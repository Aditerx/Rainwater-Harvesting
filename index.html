What is Rainwater Harvesting?

Rainwater harvesting is a technique used for collecting, storing and using rainwater for landscape irrigation and other uses. The rainwater is collected from various hard surfaces such as rooftops and/or other manmade aboveground hard surfaces. For a dry climate , rainwater harvesting can make a huge beneficial impact on the environment

The befenits of using Rainwater:

  • Reduce water bills
  • Provide an alternative supply during water restrictions
  • Maintain a green, healthy garden
  • Reduce mains water by 100%
  • Decreases storm water runoff
  • Helping to reduce local flooding and scouring of creeks
